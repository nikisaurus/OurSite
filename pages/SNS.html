"A fun way to improve leadership and public speaking skills with a tight and consistent small group who has the power to impact the HNA community. Definitely take this class." - Casey Pelz '15
Sport and Spirit Officers
Credits:
2 semesters, 1 credit per semester

Prerequisites:
Application, approval of Athletic Director, and selection in late spring.

Under the guidance of the Athletic Director, officers will coordinate the all-school Jogathon, organize spirit point games, lead weekly rep meetings, publicize athletic events, conduct sports award ceremonies, attend athletic events, design and create posters, and encourage school spirit. (from the course catalog)
