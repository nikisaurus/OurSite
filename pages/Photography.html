Digital Photography
Credits:
1 semester, 1 credit

Prerequisites:
None.

This course is designed to teach students about the wide world of digital photography. It begins with a discussion of the history of photography and how this art form has evolved. Students will learn about functions of the camera, including shutter speed and lens aperture. Students will learn to enhance, crop, and manipulate images in Adobe Photoshop Elements 9.0. Each student will create a final professional portfolio by the end of the term. (from the course reader)
