Media Studies I: Film
Credits:
1 semester, 1 credit

Prerequisites:
None.

Students in the course will gain a foundation in the history, theory, and criticism of moving images by watching a number of films in class, from classics such as Vertigo and It’s a Wonderful Life to contemporary films such as Pirates of the Caribbean and Slumdog Millionaire. Additionally, students will be introduced to the central debates, topics, and methods in the field of cinema and media studies. (from the course catalog)
