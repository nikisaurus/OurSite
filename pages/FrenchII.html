French II
Credits:
2 semesters, 1 credit per semester

Prerequisites:
Completion of French I.

This course is a continuation of French I with more emphasis on grammar and vocabulary. Emphasis is placed on the improvement and refinement of language skills such as listening, speaking, and writing. Students will gain a deeper understanding of Francophone culture and utilize language creatively in real life scenarios. (from the course catalog)
