Media Studies II: Video Production
Credits:
1 semester, 1 credit

Prerequisites:
None. While this class integrates and builds upon the foundation laid in Media Studies I, students are not required to take Media Studies I.

This course will focus on the techniques and the aesthetics of shooting, lighting, and editing film and video. Emphasis is placed on hands-on production experience (creating commercials, music videos, and short films) and the criticism and evaluation of student-generated work. (from the course catalog)
