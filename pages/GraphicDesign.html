Graphic Design
Credits:
1 semester, 1 credit

Prerequisites:
None.

This course will provide students with practice in the basic skills used in the commercial art field. Students will explore layout techniques, color and design theory, and letter formations and logos. Students will create artwork using the elements and principles of design. The major class projects will lead students through the important stages in the process of creating professional graphic designs. Students will learn how to execute designs using Adobe Photoshop 6.0 and will create a digital portfolio. (from the course catalog)
