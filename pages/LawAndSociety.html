Law and Society
Credits:
1 semester, 1 credit

Prerequisites:
None.

This course provides students with an introduction to the American legal system, focusing primarily on criminal and Constitutional law. Units dealing with the investigation process, trial procedure, Constitutional framework, the American court system, and dispute mediation will be included. Methodology for this class includes class debate, case-study examinations, mock trials, and legal research. (from the course catalog)
