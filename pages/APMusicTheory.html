Advanced Placement Music Theory
Credits:
2 semesters, 1 credit per semester

Prerequisites:
Application; signature of teacher.

This class is equivalent to a first-year college course in music theory, in which serious music students learn to recognize, understand, and describe the basic materials and processes of music that are heard or presented in a score. Throughout this course, students will develop the following skills in preparation for the AP Music Theory Exam: ear-training, score analysis, composition, dictation, and sight-singing. The ultimate goal of this class is to give dedicated music students a chance to dramatically improve their level of musicianship and musical understanding. (from the course catalog)
