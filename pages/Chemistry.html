AChemistry
Credits:
2 semesters, 1 credit per semester

Prerequisites:
None.

TThis is a college-preparatory laboratory course that provides an introduction to chemistry. The course studies the composition and structure of matter and its changes with accompanying energy changes. Electronic structure, chemical bonding, chemical formulas and equations, molecular geometries, stoichiometry, solutions, and acids and bases will also be covered.(from the course catalog)

Q: What is the most challenging?
A: Applying algebra to real life situations.
Q: What is the most popular in the course?
A: Learning about reactions, hands on labs, using fire.
