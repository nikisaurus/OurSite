Small Instrument Ensembles
Credits:
2 semesters, 1/2 credit per semester

Prerequisites:
Audition; signature of teacher.

Small instrumental ensembles are performance-oriented groups that meet once a week. Various musical styles will be studied. Groups will be formed according to student ability and interest, and may include jazz combo, rock band, flute choir, clarinet choir, string quartet, and other chamber music ensembles. Students are responsible for providing their own instruments. (from the course catalog)
