Acting II
Credits:
1 semester, 1 credit

Prerequisites:
Acting I; signature of teacher.

This course furthers the exploration of acting started in Acting I through continuing work in more advanced acting techniques, use of increasingly complex material, exposure to advanced techniques, and creation of a student-generated piece of theatre or film. Students will begin working with heightened language and will further develop their voices and physicality. (from the course catalog)
