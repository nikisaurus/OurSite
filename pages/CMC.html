“CMC is my favorite class ever. Not only do you get to participate in school life at a whole new level, but we have the opportunity to help strengthen the spiritual aspect of the HNA community through planning liturgies, retreats, and service.” – Katherine Sarlitto ‘15
Campus Ministry
Credits:
2 semesters, 1 credit per semester

Prerequisites:
Application; interview by Campus Minister; selection in late spring.

Under the guidance of the Campus Minister and the Service Coordinator, the members of the team will be responsible for planning all-school liturgies and reconciliation services (Advent and Lenten seasons), recruiting and training liturgical ministers, planning and conducting retreats, organizing Christmas Baskets, publicizing and organizing all-school outreach projects as well as individual opportunities for service. In addition, Campus Ministry and Community officers will be active, visible members of the student body by planning assemblies and prayer services, serving as leaders for school functions, and helping to create a community of faith and service. (from the course catalog)
