Art 2-D: Drawing and Painting
Credits:
2 semesters, 1 credit per semester

Prerequisites:
None.

This is a year-long, interdisciplinary art course that involves painting, drawing and graphic design. Students will explore a variety of two-dimensional techniques, such as charcoal drawing, watercolor, and acrylic painting. Students will also learn how to integrate digital media into paintings and drawings using Adobe Photoshop. This class will be open to beginning and experienced students. (from the course catalog)
