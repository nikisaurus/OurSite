NEEDS A QUOTE
Preparation Course for College Entrance Tests
Credits:
None

Prerequisites:
Fee of $125

This course offers directed preparation for College Board tests such as the SAT, PSAT, or ACT. The course is designed to develop strategies for approaching problems in both the verbal and mathematical areas. It includes frequent practice problems and tests in order to reduce test anxiety. This course will meet either two times per week for a six-week block of time or for six Saturday sessions. This course is typically offered twice per year in the fall and winter. Application materials are emailed home in the parent newsletter. Registration fee and materials are approximately $125; price is subject to change. Course is limited to first come, first served, with priority going to 11th and 12th graders. Registration information and forms are distributed in the parent newsletters. Contact the Academic Office for more information.
