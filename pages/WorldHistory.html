World History
Credits:
2 semesters, 1 credit per semester

Prerequisites:
None.

This is a survey of world history from the Middle Ages to contemporary times. The impact of various elements of human activity – economic, political, social, philosophical, geographical, and cultural – on the history of the world is stressed.(from the course catalog)

Q: What is the most challenging?
A: Note-taking, Group work, and writing.
Q: What is the most popular in the course?
A: Guild projects and anything that connects to today.
