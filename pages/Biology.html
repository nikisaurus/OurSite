Biology
Credits:
2 semesters, 1 credit per semester

Prerequisites:
None.

TThis course introduces students to biology, the study of life. This course will enhance student understanding of the scientific method, the molecular and cellular aspects of living things, the structure and function of living organisms, genetics, evolution, plant and animal diversity, classification, and ecological inter-relationships. This course will also serve as a strong foundation for AP Biology. (from the course catalog)

Q: What is the most challenging?
A: Fast pace, challenging content, applying difficult concepts to a test.
Q: What is the most popular in the course?
A: Genetics, lab work, immune system, evolution.
